# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-20

## Overview
SWARMZ provides operator-facing runtime controls. This policy describes what data may be processed by the app wrapper and connected services.

## Data We Process
- Runtime interaction data (commands, status requests, action responses)
- Technical diagnostics (crash/error logs)
- Optional account/contact details if provided by operator workflows

## Data Sources
- Data entered by the operator in app screens
- Data returned from SWARMZ backend APIs
- Device/platform diagnostics from Android/iOS runtime

## How Data Is Used
- To provide runtime control features
- To maintain reliability, debugging, and security
- To improve service quality and prevent abuse

## Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers required to operate the service.

## Data Retention
Operational logs are retained only as needed for service operation, debugging, compliance, and security.

## Security
Reasonable technical and organizational controls are used to protect data in transit and at rest.

## Your Choices
You may request deletion or correction of your personal data where applicable.

## Children
The app is not intended for children under 13.

## Contact
For privacy requests, contact: support@swarmz.local
