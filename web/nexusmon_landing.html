THE DOCTRINE.

LAW 01
THE OPERATOR IS SOVEREIGN
One operator. One organism. Non-transferable bond earned through use. The system exists to serve the operator, not the platform, not the model provider, not the cloud.
LAW 02
AUTONOMY IS EARNED, NOT GRANTED
You don't start with autonomous execution. You earn it. E-rank first. S-rank only when the organism has proven it deserves the latitude. No shortcuts. No config flags that bypass the ladder.
LAW 03
IF YOU CAN'T REPLAY IT, IT DIDN'T HAPPEN
Every A/S-rank mission requires explicit approval. Every output is versioned with its exact input. Every decision has an audit trail. Reproducibility is the only proof of legitimacy.
LAW 04
EVOLUTION IS CAPABILITY, NOT PERSONALITY
Traits are permissions. Stages are access levels. DORMANT cannot spawn workers. SOVEREIGN can chain them. That is the only difference that matters. The organism has no feelings — it has an expanding operational envelope.
LAW 05
INFRASTRUCTURE BEFORE AESTHETICS
The cockpit is real only if the engine is real. The evolution is meaningful only if the permissions are enforced. If the backend doesn't hold, the frontend is theater.
LAW 06
OPEN BY DESIGN
Workers are plugins. Missions are templates. Evolution paths are modules. If a contributor cannot extend the system without editing core code, the architecture has failed.
LAW 07
LOCAL FIRST
All data belongs to the operator. No cloud dependency required. The organism runs on your hardware, stores to your disk, answers to no one but you.
